ADD'n'Chat


Removing the stigma and supporting our neurodivergent community.

The "What"


TimeChat is an educational platform and
social networking space for those with ADHD.
Our goal is to support people with the diagnosis
by providing them with a variety of resources to help
them better manage their symptoms and will also
provide those who are not as familiar with
ADHD resources to teach them about what the diagnosis
entails and how to better support those around them.






The "Why"

We cannot stand by while our neurodivergent
community struggles with challenges,
and we as a society must do everything we can to support them.

Everyone deserves the chance to make connections without fear.
